Detailed Description

A mirror of the Profiler struct to store all the statistics of a profiler and the tracks it contains (thanks to Profile::ProfileTrackResult).

In this mirror, the tracks are packed at the beginning of the array to avoid having to iterate over all the tracks that might not have been used.

Captures the statistics of a Profile::Profiler.

It will effectively assign or compute the values of all the member variables of this struct. In particular, it will fill the tracks array with the statistics of the Profile::ProfileTracks of the profiler that have been used.
